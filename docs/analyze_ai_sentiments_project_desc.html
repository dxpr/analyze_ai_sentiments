Comprehensive AI-Powered Content Analysis

Gain deep insights into your content's effectiveness with multi-dimensional AI analysis covering trust, objectivity, audience targeting, and reading levels to optimize engagement and impact.

You need AI Sentiments Analysis if

  • You want to optimize content tone and approach for different audiences
  • Your content needs to meet specific credibility or objectivity standards
  • You publish content for diverse audience segments with different reading levels
  • You need systematic analysis of content effectiveness across multiple dimensions

Advanced Analysis Features

  • Flexible Sentiments Configuration

    Add, remove, and reorder sentiments dimensions through an intuitive UI. Enable or disable specific analysis types per content type for targeted insights.

  • Multi-Dimensional Content Evaluation

    Pre-configured sentiments dimensions provide comprehensive content assessment:

    • Trust & Credibility - From Promotional to Authoritative
    • Objectivity & Bias - From Opinion-Based to Fact-Based
    • Audience Vibe Check - From Gen Z to Boomer targeting
    • CEFR Reading Level - From A1 Beginner to C2 Proficient
  • Intuitive Visual Feedback

    Visual gauge displays provide instant, easy-to-interpret analysis results for content creators.

  • Unified Integration

    Seamless integration with the Analyze framework ensures consistent reporting across all analysis tools.

Quick Setup Guide

  1. Configure AI provider settings at /admin/config/ai/providers
  2. Configure and order sentiments at /admin/config/analyze/sentiments
  3. Enable/disable the analyzer per content type at /admin/config/content/analyze-settings
  4. Enable/disable specific sentiments per content type at /admin/structure/types/manage/{content-type}
  5. Configure permissions at /admin/people/permissions#module-analyze_ai_sentiments
  6. Access sentiments analysis through the Analyze tab on content
